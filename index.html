About Arantis

Arantis Ltd is a forward-thinking technical consultancy based in the UK. We specialise in guiding organisations through their digital transformation journeys. Our core belief is that technology, when expertly applied, can unlock significant potential and drive business success.

With a wealth of experience in modern IT paradigms, we provide pragmatic advice and hands-on expertise. We're passionate about helping our clients adopt best practices, optimise their operations, and build resilient, scalable systems for the future.

Our Services

Cloud Architecture

We design robust, secure, and scalable cloud architectures tailored to your specific business needs, leveraging platforms like AWS, Azure, and GCP. Our focus is on creating cost-efficient and future-proof solutions.

Cloud Migrations

Seamlessly migrate your existing applications, data, and infrastructure to the cloud. We handle the entire migration process, from planning and execution to post-migration support, ensuring minimal disruption to your business.

DevOps Implementation

Accelerate your software delivery lifecycle and improve operational efficiency by adopting DevOps principles. We help implement CI/CD pipelines, automation, infrastructure-as-code, and monitoring solutions.

From concept to deployment, we build bespoke software solutions designed to address your unique challenges and opportunities. Our agile development process ensures we deliver high-quality software that meets your requirements.

Interim / Fractional CTO Support

Providing experienced technology leadership on an interim or fractional basis. We help guide your tech strategy, manage development teams, and make critical decisions to align technology with your business goals during periods of transition or growth.

Legacy System Modernisation

Transform your outdated legacy systems into agile, scalable, and maintainable solutions. We assess your current applications and infrastructure, devising a modernisation roadmap that minimises risk and maximises business value.
